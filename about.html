1997

  1. Diesel wins the Grand Prix at Cannes International adverting festival. A massive flagship store is opened in San Francisco.
  2. Diesel focuses on USA and UK and opens new markets such as Hong Kong, Middle East and South Africa.
  3. At the end of the year Diesel owns 19 stores in 7 countries.

1999

DieselStyleLab catwalk show held in London. Diesel Kids and 55DSL became two separate business units. A new interior design concept is presented in Milan and Harrods reflecting the development of the brand.
